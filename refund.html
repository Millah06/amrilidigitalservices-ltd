Refund Policy

Effective Date: 17th February, 2026

At Amrili Digital Services Ltd, we are committed to providing reliable and efficient digital services. This Refund Policy explains the circumstances under which refunds are issued.

1. Non-Refundable Services

All successful transactions, including but not limited to airtime, data purchases, bill payments, and other digital services, are final and non-refundable.

2. Refundable Cases

  • Failed Transactions: If a transaction is debited from your account but the service was not delivered.
  • Duplicate Charges: If your account was charged more than once for the same transaction.

3. Refund Process

  • Customers must report issues within 24 hours of the transaction.
  • Provide proof of payment (e.g., screenshot, transaction reference) via info@amrilidigitalservices.com.
  • Approved refunds will be processed back to your original payment method within 5–10 business days.

4. Exceptions

We do not process refunds for incorrect details provided by the customer (e.g., wrong phone number, meter number). Please verify all information before completing a transaction.
